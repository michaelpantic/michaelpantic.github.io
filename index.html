Hi, I'm Michael! I am currently a Senior Researcher at the Autonomous Systems Lab (ASL) at ETH Zurich, where I am leading our work on aerial robots.

I completed a PhD in at the Autonomous Systems Lab where I was working on Navigation and Autonomy for Aerial Robots, a topic which brought me (and the robots) to places ranging from deserts, disaster scenarios, construction sites to high-alpine glaciers!

Previously, I spent some time at EPF Lausanne, where I was working on on-orbit navigation for space-junk capturing satellites (what later became clearspace.today).

Before all of that, I worked in IT, on roles from large enterprise Network Engineer to Software Developer/Architect for service-oriented enterprise software (at what today is bithawk.ch, but back then it was Bison Systems / IT Services).
